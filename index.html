Floodead — Half-Flooded Office Survival Horror

A reskin of FlooDead into "Wanaka's own office building, flooded by a rainstorm, colleagues turned infected" — a three-chapter first-person horror.

checkpoint chk_0Mm2ESM6aNXVR0m Editor / Playable →

What was built

A full-featured build. You play a nameless employee exploring the half-flooded building and facing colleagues turned into infected — real teammates from the wall roster. You can put them down, or save them with serum; the more you save, the better the ending. The Chapter 3 boss is Chai Junyang (the director) himself.

Core Loop Fidelity

  • ✅First-person exploration of the flooded building across three chapters (office floor 4800² → underground server room → research level / contamination source)
  • ✅Melee combat: F attacks the infected; killing drops a badge revealing the real teammate's identity
  • ✅Multi-enemy AI (findAll + per-enemy state via WeakMap)
  • ✅Moral choice: kill (F) vs. rescue with serum (G); rescue count drives 4 endings (Homeward ≥5 / Redemption ≥3 / Glimmer ≥1 / Survivor 0)
  • ✅Winding maps: chapters 2–3 rebuilt from straight corridors into S-curve zigzags via staggered partition walls; enemies met one per leg (no entrance swarm)
  • ✅Horror pacing: sudden-approach jump scare + proximity heartbeat that quickens as enemies close in + occasional power flickers
  • ✅Sound: hit thud, dialogue blip, horror chase drone, heartbeat, sparse dripping (numpy-synthesized + S3-uploaded)
  • ✅Departed-colleague rescue cavalry (all three chapters): when near death, Dai Weiheng / Zhao Meichen teleport in, suppress an enemy and heal you
  • ◐Art: swapped in a CC-BY server rack + custom audio; the rest is mostly catalog placeholders (animated characters kept to preserve their animation clips)
  • ✗Real-window balance validation: difficulty / feel / fidelity to the original need a director playthrough to judge

Gaps vs the Original

  • ◐Art fidelity: still mostly catalog models; water surface / lighting / furniture / corpse props could be pushed further (the Sketchfab→S3 pipeline is proven, it just wasn't done at scale)
  • ◐Animated characters not re-modeled: swapping would lose the Zombie_* animation clips and break the framework, so catalog zombies were kept
  • ✗Balance / fidelity to the original: a director's live judgment; needs a full playthrough to settle
  • ✗Chapter 1 not made winding; boss fight is HP-bar melee; single enemy type; no between-chapter checkpoints

How to Play the Demo

Editor → Preview mode. Goal: descend through three chapters → put down or rescue the infected colleagues (badges reveal real names) → reach the blue exit. The more you save, the better the ending (save 5 = the everyone-survives "Homeward" ending). When you're near death, a departed colleague rushes in to save you. The Chapter 3 boss is Chai Junyang — you can defeat him or cure him with serum.
